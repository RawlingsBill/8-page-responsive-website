Style

Colour Scheme

Used adobe colour wheel to find colour scheme. Complementary dark blue and beige were the two mian colours used. With a lighter blue for some highlightiung and trim.These colours were chosen for the contrast they provide and the dark theme as this is a Astronomy website.

Fonts

Raleway font is used throughout the website. Sans serif font is used where raleway is not available. Raleway was chosen as it is easy to read and contrasts well. Sans serif as these fonts are generally easier to read on screens than serif. For the form calibri font is used as it takes up less space but remains legible.

Images
